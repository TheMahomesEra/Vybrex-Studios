## Idea 1: Dark Forge Futurism
**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk Brutalism meets Digital Craftsmanship
**Core Principles:** Raw power, neon contrast, asymmetric tension, motion-driven storytelling
**Color Philosophy:** Near-black base (#050510) with electric violet (#7C3AED) and neon cyan (#06B6D4) as primary glows; hot coral (#FF6B35) as a disruptive accent that signals energy and urgency
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed asymmetric sections with diagonal clip-paths cutting between sections; hero is a split-screen with image left, text right; services use a staggered masonry grid
**Signature Elements:** Glowing neon borders on cards; animated particle field in hero; scanline texture overlay on dark sections
**Interaction Philosophy:** Every hover triggers a glow pulse; CTAs have a "forge spark" animation; scroll triggers section reveals with upward slide
**Animation:** Framer Motion entrance animations (fade + slide up); hero particles animate continuously; service cards glow on hover
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk Bold for headlines (futuristic, geometric); Inter for body text; large display numbers for stats

## Idea 3: Signal & Frequency
**Design Movement:** Tech Minimalism + Audio-Visual Kinetics
**Core Principles:** Sound-inspired geometry, precision spacing, vibrant restraint, motion as language
**Color Philosophy:** Absolute black (#000000) base; single primary electric violet (#7C3AED); cyan (#06B6D4) for secondary; gold (#F59E0B) for premium accents — used sparingly for maximum impact
**Layout Paradigm:** Vertical scroll with full-width section breaks; hero has animated waveform background; services in a 3-column grid with icon-first design; process section uses a horizontal timeline
**Signature Elements:** Animated audio waveform in hero; thin neon line separators between sections; numbered service cards with large display numerals
**Interaction Philosophy:** Waveform animates on scroll; service cards expand on hover to reveal description; CTA has a "signal pulse" ripple effect
**Animation:** Waveform continuously animates; section transitions use horizontal wipe; stats count up on scroll into view
**Typography System:** Orbitron for hero display text (sci-fi, futuristic); Space Grotesk for section headings; Inter for body
</text>
